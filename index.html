The Process

We do the work.
You enjoy the car.

From pinpointing the exact vehicle you want to shaking hands on a deal, Skippr manages the entire car-buying experience on your behalf.

01

Tell Us What You Want

We onboard you and lock in every detail — make, model, trim, color inside and out. No vague requests; just your perfect car.

02

We Search the Market

We research inventory within 100 miles of you first, then expand outward. We handle out-of-state dealers too — distance isn't an obstacle.

03

We Negotiate Everything

Doc fees, dealer add-ons, shipping, titling — we push on all of it. Cash or finance, trade-in or not, the rate is the same.

04

You Approve the Deal

We won't shake hands without you. Once we get $1,000+ off (Tier 1) or our full target (Tier 2), we bring the deal to you for final sign-off.
