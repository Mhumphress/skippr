2. Description of Services

Skippr is a car buying concierge service that provides professional representation to clients during the vehicle purchasing process. We negotiate with dealerships, manage dealer communications, review finance and lease documents, and guide you through every step of the car buying process. Skippr does not purchase vehicles on your behalf, hold title to any vehicle, act as a licensed automobile dealer or broker, or provide legal or financial advice. All final purchasing decisions remain exclusively with the Client.

Skippr offers the following service tiers:

  • Expert Advocate ($1,000): Comprehensive vehicle selection consultation; full negotiation on vehicle price, dealer fees, add-ons, and shipping costs; finance and lease office representation; final document review; and trade-in equity optimization.
  • White Glove Concierge ($2,500): All Expert Advocate services plus priority service; exact color and specification sourcing; specific options and packages; out-of-state transport and logistics management; and a complete hands-off experience from first consult to final signature.

5. Refund Policy

Skippr is committed to transparency and accountability. Our refund policy is built around two specific, measurable commitments established with every client prior to the start of work: a timeframe commitment to securing a deal on the specified vehicle, and a discount target commitment. The process for establishing and agreeing to these commitments is described below.

How the Parameters Are Established:

Following the initial consultation, and at the time the invoice is sent, Skippr will provide the client with a written overview detailing: (a) the specific vehicle the client wishes to purchase, including all relevant information pertaining to said vehicle; (b) the timeframe within which Skippr commits to securing a qualifying deal; and (c) the discount target Skippr intends to pursue based on current market conditions for that vehicle, thus making it a qualifying deal. This written overview, along with these Terms of Service and the Privacy Policy, will be delivered to the client prior to payment.

By submitting payment, the client confirms that they have received, reviewed, and agreed to the timeframe and discount parameters communicated in the written overview. Payment serves as the client's binding acknowledgment of these parameters and constitutes full acceptance of the terms under which Skippr's performance will be evaluated for refund purposes. No additional signature is required for these parameters to be enforceable.

Work begins immediately upon receipt of payment. No work will commence prior to payment being received in full.

Refund Triggers:

  • Timeframe Overrun (50% Refund): If Skippr fails to secure a qualifying deal within the timeframe stated in the written overview, the client is entitled to request a 50% refund. The request must be submitted in writing to support@skippr.us within seven (7) calendar days of the timeframe expiration.
  • Discount Target Not Met (50% Refund): If Skippr secures a deal at a discount below the target stated in the written overview, the client is entitled to request a 50% refund. The request must be submitted in writing to support@skippr.us within seven (7) calendar days of the deal being presented to the client.
  • Both Conditions Not Met (100% Refund): If Skippr both exceeds the stated timeframe and fails to meet the stated discount target, the client is entitled to a full 100% refund.

Refund Exclusions:

Refund requests will not be honored under the following circumstances:

  • The client independently contacts, negotiates with, or communicates directly with any dealership Skippr is actively working with on the client's behalf.
  • The client declines a deal that meets or exceeds both the stated timeframe and the stated discount target without a substantive reason.
  • The client provides inaccurate, incomplete, or misleading information that materially affects Skippr's ability to perform.
  • The client has already executed a final purchase agreement, lease agreement, or retail installment contract at the dealership.

All refund requests must be submitted in writing to support@skippr.us. Approved refunds will be processed within ten (10) business days to the original payment method. The refund policy described in this section represents the client's sole financial remedy under this Agreement.
